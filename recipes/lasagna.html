Lasagna Recipe

This classic Italian dish layers sheets of pasta with a rich meat or vegetable sauce, often including ricotta cheese and mozzarella. Baked until bubbly and golden brown, lasagna is a hearty and comforting meal perfect for a family dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat or Vegetable Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Sauce: Make your chosen sauce (meat or vegetable).
  2. Cook the Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Assemble the Layers: In a baking dish, layer the sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese (if using), and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until the dish is full.
  4. Bake: Cover the dish with foil and bake in a preheated oven. Uncover and bake for a few more minutes to brown the top.
  5. Rest: Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
